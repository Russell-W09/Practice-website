Racket

A specialized, lightweight, and somewhat durable tool designed for striking a shuttlecock across the net.


Shuttlecock

An object with an open conical shape designed to be hit across the court. Usually made of feathers, plastic, or hybird materials.


Shoes

A specialized indoor court footwear designed for quick, explosive, and repetitive movements on the court
