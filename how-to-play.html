how to play

for more info, click on the icons

Shots

  • Clear - a high shot towards the opponent's backcourt
  • Drop - a soft shot to land behind the opponent's net
  • Lift - a defensive shot to the opponent's backcourt
  • Smash - a high power shot aimed downwards for a kill
  • Drive - a flat shot played straight across the net
  • Net - a tight shot to bounce the shuttle over the net

Footwork

  • Split step - a small movement enabling a change in direction early
  • Lunge - a movement of leg when moving forward the knee up to 90 degrees
  • Chasse step - a movement of bringing your leg towards the other leg
  • Pivot - a small moving action to turn your body sideways
  • Dive - a hail mary action to return a difficult shot

Rules

  • Serves - the winner serves diagonally, serve on right when points are even and serve on left when points are odd
  • Points - first to 21 wins, with win by 2 at 20 points for each player
  • Hits - can only hit the shuttlecock once
  • Single courts - the innermost sidelines, and the whole backcourt
  • Double court - the outermost sidelines, and the whole backcourt after serving
